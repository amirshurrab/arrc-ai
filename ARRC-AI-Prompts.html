Battle-ready prompt templates for the three highest-leverage tools — Claude for agentic delivery, Gamma for decks, NotebookLM for grounded research. Copy, paste, swap the placeholders, go.

💡 Replace anything in [BRACKETS] with your project specifics before running.

Claude — delegate a whole workflow

These are delegation briefs, not one-line prompts. Give Claude the goal, the steps, and the guardrails — then let it work. Each ends with a human approval gate before anything leaves the building.

🛠️ Best in an agentic setup (e.g. Cowork / Claude with tools & connectors) so Claude can actually read files, search, and produce .docx/.pptx/.xlsx — but they work in a normal chat too if you paste the documents in.

Gamma — generate a polished deck from a prompt

Paste one of these into Gamma's "Generate" box. Be specific about audience, structure, slide count and design — Gamma rewards detail.

🎨 After it generates: refine in Gamma's editor. If you need PowerPoint, export then fix brand fonts/spacing — and remember Gamma's Arabic-script handling is weaker, so review RTL slides.

NotebookLM — ask questions grounded in your own sources

NotebookLM only answers from documents you upload, and cites the exact passage — so it won't invent facts. Upload your RFP, regulations, reports or interview transcripts first, then run these.

📂 Upload first: the project's regulations, RFP, prior reports, and interview transcripts (Arabic or English). Then ask. Mind client-confidentiality rules on what you upload.
