The LGN solution suite

Five AI products for the learning ecosystem

Each maps to a real need across schools, ministries, training providers and the labour market — and to ARRC's Human Capital & Education practice.

🧑‍🏫

AI Teaching Assistant

Teachers · Trainers · Students

An always-on AI tutor and educator aide — lesson planning, instant grading and feedback, and personalised 1:1 tutoring in Arabic and English, grounded in the curriculum.

Why it matters: scales high-quality teaching across large cohorts, frees educators from admin, and supports national education-transformation targets.
🗂️

Asset Management Systems

Institutions · Ministries

AI-driven management of learning and operational assets — content libraries, devices, licences and facilities — with usage analytics and predictive maintenance.

Why it matters: maximises the utilisation and ROI of large education investments and gives decision-makers data for capacity planning.
🧒

AI Kids Club

Schools · Families · Youth programs

A safe, gamified AI learning space for children — creativity, coding, languages and AI literacy — with strong guardrails, age-appropriate content and parental controls.

Why it matters: builds early AI literacy for the next generation — a visible flagship for national youth-capability ambitions.
🪪

AI Passport

Learners · Employers · Regulators

A verifiable digital credential and skills portfolio that records a learner's competencies and AI proficiency — portable and trusted across institutions and employers.

Why it matters: links education to the labour market with portable proof of capability — a natural complement to ARRC's labour-market analytics work.
📚

AI Course Catalogue

Learners · L&D · Workforce planners

An AI-curated catalogue that recommends personalised learning paths — matched to each learner's goals and to real, current labour-market demand.

Why it matters: aligns upskilling with the skills the economy actually needs, turning training spend into measurable workforce outcomes.
🚀

UPGRADEjourney™

The methodology underneath

LGN's flagship, trademarked upskilling methodology — engineered to break psychological barriers to technology and build lasting digital confidence and leadership.

Why it matters: the proven framework that makes the five solutions stick — adoption and confidence, not just tools.
